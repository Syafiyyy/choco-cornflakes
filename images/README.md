# Product Images Folder

Place your product images here with the following names:

## Required Images:

1. **cornflakes-salted-egg.jpg** (or .png)
   - Image of your Cornflakes Salted Egg product
   - Recommended size: 300x200 pixels
   - Clean, professional background

2. **combo-chocojar.jpg** (or .png)
   - Image of your Combo ChocoJar product
   - Recommended size: 300x200 pixels
   - Clean, professional background

## After Adding Images:

Once you place your images in this folder, they will automatically appear on your website at:
- Cornflakes Salted Egg: images/cornflakes-salted-egg.jpg
- Combo ChocoJar: images/combo-chocojar.jpg

## Tips:
- Use good lighting to show product details
- Keep background simple and clean
- Ensure images are high quality but optimized for web (under 200KB each)
- JPG format works best for product photos
